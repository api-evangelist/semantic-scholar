Along with our rebrand, we’re excited to debut a new release note process. Because we’re making regular updates and new asset roll-outs in our open ecosystem of OLMo , Dolma , and associated tools, we want to keep the community in the loop as new and updated tools become available. We’ll be posting templated release notes to X , our website, and sending them to our newsletter subscribers to ensure users get the latest updates as soon as possible.
